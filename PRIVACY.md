# Privacy Policy for FindTrace

**Last updated: March 15, 2026**

## Overview

FindTrace ("the App") is a GPS track recording application for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

The App collects the following data:

- **Precise Location Data**: GPS coordinates, speed, altitude, and heading from your device's location services. This data is used solely to record your travel tracks and determine which cities you have visited.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored **locally on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- No data is transmitted to any external server.
- No data is shared with any third party.
- No analytics or tracking services are used.

## Data Retention

- Data remains on your device as long as the App is installed.
- Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all stored data.
- When you back up your device via iCloud or iTunes, App data may be included in the backup.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools. The App uses Apple's built-in MapKit for map display and CoreLocation for GPS data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
