# FindTrace Support

## FAQ

### Why does FindTrace need "Always" location permission?

FindTrace records your GPS tracks continuously in the background. Without "Always" permission, tracking stops when you leave the app.

### Does FindTrace drain my battery?

FindTrace uses battery optimization techniques including switching to low-power mode when you are stationary. Typical battery impact is minimal.

### Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

### Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all tracks and city data.

### Can I transfer data to a new phone?

Currently, data does not sync across devices. If you restore an iCloud/iTunes backup to a new phone, your data may be included.

### How does city light-up work?

When you stay in a city for more than 30 minutes, it is automatically "lit up" and added to your city map. Passing through a city (e.g., by train) does not count.

### Why are my tracks slightly offset on the map?

In China, map tiles use the GCJ-02 coordinate system. FindTrace automatically converts GPS coordinates for correct display. Outside China, no conversion is applied.
